How to Use the Invoice VAT Calculator

Step 1: Enter Details

  • Amount: Enter the subtotal or item price. Must be a positive number.
  • Percent (%): Defaults to 100%. Represents the portion of the subtotal to calculate. Can be changed.
  • VAT (%): Defaults to 12%. This is the tax rate applied to the total. Can be changed.

Step 2: Calculate

Click the Calculate button or press Enter while focused on an input field. The app validates your input and shows an alert if values are missing or invalid.

Step 3: View Results

  • Total Amount: Calculated as Amount × (Percent / 100)
  • Net of VAT: The subtotal without VAT, calculated as Total ÷ (1 + VAT/100)
  • Less of VAT: The VAT portion of the total, calculated as Total − Net of VAT
  • Amount in Words: The total amount is converted into words and displayed in ALL CAPS.

Example:

Input Value
Amount 1,000.00
Percent (%) 100
VAT (%) 12

Output:

Result Value
Total Amount 1,000.00
Net of VAT 892.86
Less of VAT 107.14
Amount in Words ONE THOUSAND

Tips

  • Always enter numeric values; empty or invalid inputs will show an alert.
  • Percent and VAT fields can be left blank to use the default values (100% and 12%).
  • The "Amount in Words" field will auto-expand for long totals.
  • For multiple items, sum them first and input the subtotal as Amount.
